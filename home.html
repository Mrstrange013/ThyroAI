Common Myths vs Facts

Myth: Thyroid problems only affect older women.
Fact: Can affect men, infants, teens, and all ages.
Myth: A lump always means cancer.
Fact: 95% of thyroid nodules are benign.
Myth: Extra iodine always helps.
Fact: Too much iodine can worsen Hashimoto’s.

What is the Thyroid?

The thyroid is a butterfly-shaped gland at the base of your neck. It is the engine of your metabolism — regulating energy use, temperature, heart rate, and weight.

Key Disorders

Hypothyroidism

Underactive – insufficient hormone production

  • Weight gain
  • Fatigue
  • Cold intolerance

Hyperthyroidism

Overactive – excess hormone production

  • Weight loss
  • Anxiety & tremors
  • Heat intolerance
